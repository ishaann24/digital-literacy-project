For this task, I used Canva to create a one-page infographic about digital literacy for students. Canva is a free and easy-to-use design platform that provides templates, icons, and layout tools, which helped me design the visual resource in a clear and attractive way.

My design explains the concept of digital literacy and highlights why it is important for students in the modern digital world. The infographic includes several sections. The first section explains what digital literacy means, which is the ability to effectively and safely use digital technologies to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information. The second section shows useful digital tools for students, such as Google Workspace, Canva, Grammarly, and Notion, which help in writing, organizing notes, and creating presentations. Another section focuses on safe internet practices, including using strong passwords, being cautious of phishing, checking privacy settings, and thinking carefully before posting online.

One interesting part of creating this infographic was choosing icons and colors that make the information visually appealing and easy to understand. The challenging part was organizing all the information into a single slide while keeping the design neat and not overcrowded.
